What We're Happy to Help With

Here are some of the common reasons people reach out to us:

  • Reading Order Questions: Confused about where to start or what to read next? We can help guide you.
  • Content Suggestions: Have an idea for an article or guide we should create? Let us know!
  • Corrections: Spotted an error in our content? We appreciate accuracy and welcome corrections.
  • Collaboration: Interested in contributing or collaborating? We're open to discussing opportunities.
  • General Feedback: Love the site? Have suggestions for improvement? We value all feedback.
  • Fan Discussions: Want to share your favorite moments or theories? We enjoy connecting with fellow fans.

Community Guidelines

When reaching out, we kindly ask that you:

  • Be respectful and courteous in your communications
  • Provide specific details if you're reporting an issue
  • Check existing content before asking questions that may already be answered
  • Remember that we're fellow fans who run this site as a passion project

We believe in fostering a positive community where all fans feel welcome, regardless of how long they've been following the series.
